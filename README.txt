Integrate Flizpay payment gateway seamlessly with your WooCommerce store for secure and straightforward transactions.

== Description ==

The Flizpay WooCommerce Plugin allows you to offer your customers an easy and secure payment option by integrating the Flizpay payment gateway directly into your WooCommerce store. By following three simple steps, you can start accepting payments at competitive rates without hassle.

This plugin requires a functioning WordPress site with WooCommerce installed and activated. Make sure you have a Fliz Business account and have received your clientId to utilize this payment gateway fully.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/flizpay-woocommerce` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Go to WooCommerce > Settings > Payments and click on Flizpay to configure the payment gateway settings. Enter your Flizpay clientId and Shop IBAN to start accepting payments.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do I sign up for Fliz Business? =

You can sign up for Fliz Business by visiting [Flizpay Business Sign Up](https://www.flizpay.de/).

= Where do I find my Flizpay clientId? =

Once your Fliz Business account has been approved, you can find your clientId in your account dashboard under settings.
